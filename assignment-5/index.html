A5: The Easiest Peanut Butter Cookie Recipe Ever


Yeilds: Approximately 18 small cookies


Ingredients


+ 1 cup granulated sugar


+ 1 cup creamy peanut butter


+ 1 large egg



Directions


1. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.


2. Combine all 3 ingredients in a bowl with a hand mixer on low.


3. Scoop or roll dough into balls and place 2 inches apart on a cookie sheet.


4. Press dough balls with a fork twice perpendicularly.


5. Bake for approximately 10 minutes.
